Essential Minitab Commands for Business Analytics

Learn the most useful Minitab commands and steps for analyzing business data.

1. Basic and Descriptive Statistics

Summarize data with mean, median, and standard deviation.

Stat > Basic Statistics > Display Descriptive Statistics

2. Histogram

Create a histogram to visualize data distribution.

Graph > Histogram

3. Box Plot

Use a box plot to visualize spread and detect outliers.

Graph > Boxplot

4. One-Sample T-Test

Test if a sample mean differs from a known value.

Stat > Basic Statistics > 1-Sample T

5. Two-Sample T-Test

Compare means of two independent samples.

Stat > Basic Statistics > 2-Sample T

6. Correlation Analysis

Assess the relationship between two variables.

Stat > Basic Statistics > Correlation

7. Simple Linear Regression

Analyze the relationship between independent and dependent variables.

Stat > Regression > Regression > Fit Regression Model

8. One-Way ANOVA

Compare means across multiple groups.

Stat > ANOVA > One-Way ANOVA

9. Chi-Square Test for Association

Test association between two categorical variables.

Stat > Tables > Chi-Square Test for Association

10. Time Series Plot

Plot data over time to identify trends.

Graph > Time Series Plot
