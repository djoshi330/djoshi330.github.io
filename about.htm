About the Math Resource Center (MRC)

  • Mission Statement:
    • To promote the development and enhancement of concepts and skills essential for an understanding of quantitative applications in business.
    • Provide assistance and resources to help students take the required quantitative courses:
      • AQM1000
      • AQM2000
  • What We Offer:
    • Personalized tutoring sessions
    • Workshops on key quantitative skills
    • Study materials and resources
    • A supportive learning environment
